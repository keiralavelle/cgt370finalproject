Global Mental Health Crisis

Mental health outcomes vary widely across countries, shaped by both technology use and access to care. This dashboard begins by highlighting key global patterns and relationships such as how social media use and mental health systems influence anxiety, suicide rates, and overall crisis levels. Then, you can explore patterns, differences, and unexpected trends across regions through interactive tools.
